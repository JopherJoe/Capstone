About

Senior Citizens - 20% Discount Present Senior Citizen ID, passport or other document proving that holder is at least sixty (60) years of age.

PWD (Person with Disability) - 20% Discount Present PWD ID upon ticket purchase. No ID, no discount. Discount is applicable to local and foreign PWDs. Exception: PWD ID is not required when disability is apparent i.e. passenger is an amputee

Students - 20% Discount Discount can be availed throughout the year including summer breaks, legal and special legal holidays. Present valid student ID or school registration card with student name, photo and name of school. No ID, no discount. Not eligible for discount are students in medicine proper, law, graduate courses and short-term courses.
